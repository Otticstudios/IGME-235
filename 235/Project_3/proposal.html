The Idea

The Idea is Simple. Using Circle blast as my basis I am going to make something similar to it using the basic code.
The game will be called "Speen the bean". The name and concept is based off of this Vinesauce meme where he says spin in a funny voice
Its a basic Top Down arcade game where you play as a Crocodile who does a spin attack to take down anthropromrphic green beans. Go for the high score and all that basic stuff.
This is gonna be made for web desktops where you control the Crocodile using the mouse and by pressing the space bar you do a Crash Bandicoot Styled Spin attack.
Doing the attack near the Green bean Enemies cuases them to explode and gain points.

I have no idea how I came up with this but Its funny
