Hollow Knight

Publishing Info

  • Published by: Team Cherry
  • Developed by: Team Cherry
  • Released: Feb 24, 2017

Description

Hollow Knight is a singleplayer Metroidvania that has you play as they a nameless insectoid warrior as they explore
through the ruins of Hollownest a fallen kingdom plagued by a super natural infection. Set in an interconnected world
with diverse subterrarian locations. Featuring other insectoids, both friendly and hostile. Along with numerous Bosses.
As players explore, they will unlock various upgrades for movement, their "nail" weapon, and spells.
